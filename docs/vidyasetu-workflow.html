Student Experience
How a student accesses classes, recordings and exams from one portal — no app needed.
1
Login to College Portal
+
🌐
Open the college portal from any browser — phone, tablet or laptop. No app download needed.
🔑
Enter your username and password provided by the admin at the time of enrollment.
📋
On login, the dashboard opens directly showing today's timetable and any pending exams.
Your login credentials are set up by the college admin. Contact admin if you face any login issues.
2
View Your Timetable
+
📅
The timetable shows all your classes for the day — subject, batch, and scheduled time listed clearly.
🔒
Upcoming classes show a locked JOIN button with the exact start time — you cannot join early.
📝
Any scheduled exams also appear in the timetable — you can see the subject and start time in advance.
🔒 Upcoming — Locked ✅ Active — JOIN NOW ▶ Ended — Watch Recording 📝 Exam Due — Start Exam
3
Join a Pre-Recorded Class (Phase 1)
+
🟢
At the scheduled class time, the JOIN NOW button turns green and becomes clickable — only this one active class is enabled at any time.
▶
Click JOIN NOW — the pre-recorded video uploaded by your teacher starts playing directly in the portal.
⏰
The video link expires automatically after the class window — you cannot access it before or after the scheduled time (except via Watch Recording).
✅
Attendance is marked automatically the moment you click and the video starts — no manual action needed.
If you miss the class window, the recording will be available under Watch Recording after the session ends.
4
Join a Live Class (Phase 2)
+
🟢
At the scheduled time, the JOIN NOW button activates — click it and the live class opens inside the portal. No external app or link needed.
🎥
You can see and hear your teacher live, ask questions, and interact during the session.
🔴
The session is being recorded automatically. You do not need to do anything — the recording becomes available after the class ends.
✅
Attendance is auto-marked when you join the live session.
5
Watch Past Recordings
+
▶
After a class ends, a Watch Recording button appears next to that class in your timetable.
📂
All past recordings remain accessible anytime — you can revisit any previous class for revision.
🔐
Recordings are only accessible through your portal login — they cannot be downloaded or shared outside.
6
Take an Online Exam (Phase 2)
+
📝
When your teacher schedules an exam, it appears in your timetable with a Start Exam button on the exam date and time.
⏱
Click Start Exam — the question paper opens and a countdown timer starts. The exam auto-submits when time runs out.
💾
Your answers are saved automatically as you go — no need to worry about losing progress.
🏆
Once the teacher enters the answer key, your score and answer sheet are visible in your portal — no waiting for manual correction.
Results are published only after the teacher reviews and approves them. You will see your score and the correct answers side by side.

Teacher Experience
How a teacher manages content, conducts classes, sets exams and tracks attendance — all from one dashboard.
1
Login & Teacher Dashboard
+
🌐
Open the college portal from any browser and log in with your teacher credentials set by the admin.
📊
Your dashboard shows today's scheduled classes, upcoming exams, pending actions, and recent attendance summaries.
📋
You can see which subjects and batches are assigned to you, and the full weekly timetable at a glance.
2
Upload Pre-Recorded Video (Phase 1)
+
📤
Go to your subject — click Upload Video and select the recorded lesson file from your device.
🏷
Add a title, select the batch and subject — the system links it to the timetable slot automatically.
📅
The video is stored securely and will unlock for students only at the timetable slot date and time. It expires after the class window automatically.
You can upload videos in advance — they will not be visible to students until the scheduled time.
3
Conduct a Live Class (Phase 2)
+
🟢
At the scheduled class time, a Start Class button appears on your dashboard for that subject and batch.
🎥
Click Start Class — the live video room opens directly inside your portal. No external tool or link needed. Students will see the JOIN NOW button activate on their side simultaneously.
🔴
The session is recorded automatically from the moment you start — you do not need to press any record button.
🔚
Click End Class when done — the room closes for all students. The recording is saved and made available for Watch Recording automatically.
4
Set & Publish an Exam (Phase 2)
+
📝
Go to Exam Module — click Create Exam. Select subject, batch, exam type (MCQ or descriptive) and set the duration.
➕
Add questions one by one. For MCQ — add four options and mark the correct answer (used for auto-scoring). For descriptive — add the question and marks.
📅
Set the exam date and time — it will appear in the student timetable automatically. Students cannot see the questions until the exam starts.
👁
During the exam, you can monitor who has started and who has submitted from your teacher dashboard.
Once published, the exam date cannot be changed. Make sure the timetable slot is free before scheduling.
5
Enter Answer Key & Publish Results (Phase 2)
+
🗝
After the exam window closes, go to the exam in your dashboard — click Enter Answer Key.
⚡
Once you submit the key, the system automatically calculates scores for all students who appeared — instantly, no manual checking needed.
👀
Review the auto-calculated scores on screen — you can adjust marks manually if needed before publishing.
📢
Click Publish Results — students immediately see their score and answer sheet in their portal. A batch-wise and subject-wise report is also generated for you.
6
View Attendance & Reports
+
📊
Attendance is auto-tracked for every session — pre-recorded and live. No manual marking needed at any point.
🧑‍🎓
View per-student attendance — see how many classes each student attended, by subject or by date range.
📋
Class-wise attendance summary shows the total count of students who joined each session.
⬇
All reports — attendance and exam results — can be downloaded for submission to the administration.
